FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions our clients ask.

How do I start the home buying process?

Start by browsing our property listings or contacting one of our agents for a free consultation. We'll help you understand your budget, explore financing options, and identify properties that match your criteria. Our agents guide you through every step, from initial viewing to final closing.

What are the costs involved in buying a property?

Beyond the purchase price, buyers should budget for closing costs (typically 2-5% of the price), property inspection fees, appraisal fees, and potential homeowner's insurance. Our agents provide a detailed cost breakdown specific to each property so there are no surprises.

Do I need to create an account to browse listings?

No, you can freely browse all our property listings without an account. However, creating a free account lets you save favorite properties, set up search alerts, and get personalized recommendations based on your preferences.

How can I contact an agent for a specific property?

Each property listing features a "Contact Agent" form where you can send a direct inquiry. You can also call our main office, use the live chat on our contact page, or visit us in person. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2 business hours.
